#### Actions
* Ground idle
    Do nothing, maybe play some idle animations
* Running
    Hold left or right to run in that direction
    Stick to the ground if possible
* Braking
    Slide to a a stop
    Brake faster by holding counter to current direction
#### Transitions 
* [[air]] 
    lost contact with ground
    jump input
* [[ground boost]]
    boost input
* [[attack]]
    attack input
